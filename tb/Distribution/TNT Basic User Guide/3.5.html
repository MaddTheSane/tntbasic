Music Section

The music section is where you manage the music used in your project.

TNT Basic supports the use of tracker music modules (in MOD, MED, IT, MAD, MIDI, MTM, S3M and XM formats) and MP3 music.

To import a music, select "Create new Music..." from the "Resource" menu, locate the file to import and select OK. You can also import by dragging and dropping files from the Finder. To play the music double click on it.
