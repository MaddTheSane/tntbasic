Oval

Draw an oval of specified size.

Syntax

Fill Oval left,top To right,bottom
Frame Oval left,top To right,bottom

Description

Fill Oval draws a solid oval from the co-ordinate left,top to the co-ordinate right,bottom.

Frame Oval draws a hollow oval (outline) from the co-ordinate left,top to the co-ordinate right,bottom.

If the rectangle specifying the oval is square then the oval drawn will be a circle.
