Reset Channel

Resets a moan channel

Syntax

Reset Channel
Reset Channel channelId

Description

Resetting a moan channel means clearing the program from the channel, resetting its local registers and removing any sprites or viewports that are attached to it. This puts the channel back into its start state.

channelId is the channel to reset, if you do not specify a channel then all active channels are reset. Resetting all channels also resets the global moan registers.

See also Restart Channel.
